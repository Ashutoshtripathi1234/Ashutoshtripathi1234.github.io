I am a final-year Bachelor of Technology student in Electronics Engineering at the Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology, India.

During my undergraduation, I got to opportunity to work as a research assistant at The University of Hong Kong and Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, I worked on analyzing large language models (LLMs) and designing federated learning algorithms for cross-domain tasks. My research extended to video classification, biomedical signal processing and multimodal fusion.

I have co-authored more than 10 papers for conferences such as AAAI, ICLR, MICCAI, AISTATS and journals like TMLR, IEEE TAI, IEEE TCE and Applied Soft Computing etc. (Some works are currently under review).
